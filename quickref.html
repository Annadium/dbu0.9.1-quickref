Standard Maneuvers limited by Action cost
Standard Maneuvers can only be used during your turn and use Standard Actions for their Action Cost.
Instant Manuevers
An Instant Maneuver can occur during any Character’s turn, between any Standard Maneuvers that occur.
Counter Manuevers limited by Counter Action cost
Defensive Maneuvers that are used to protect your Character from harm. Counter Maneuvers cannot be used unless you are the target of an Attacking Maneuver (unless otherwise stated) and use Counter Actions for their Action Cost.
